About the Brand

Movement That Inspires

Kia has transformed from a budget brand into a design powerhouse. The EV6 won World Car of the Year 2023, and the EV9 three-row SUV competes with the Tesla Model X at nearly half the price.

Built on the same E-GMP platform as Hyundai's IONIQ line, Kia EVs feature 800V ultra-fast charging. The EV6 GT packs 576 hp for genuine performance-car acceleration.

The newer EV3 brings Kia's electric formula to a smaller, more affordable package, while the Niro EV offers a practical crossover alternative for budget-conscious buyers.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Kia EV6 better than the IONIQ 5?

+

They share the same platform and 800V tech. The EV6 has sportier styling and sharper handling. The IONIQ 5 has more interior space and a retro design. The choice is mostly about design preference — both are excellent.

How does the EV9 compare to Tesla Model X?

+

The EV9 offers three-row seating at $56K vs Model X at $80K+. Model X has more range and the Supercharger network. EV9 has a more usable third row and 800V fast charging.

Is the Kia EV3 worth waiting for?

+

If you want Kia's EV tech in a smaller, more affordable package, yes. The EV3 targets ~300 miles of range at ~$35K — strong value against the Chevy Equinox EV and VW ID.4.
