About the Brand

Luxury, Elevated

Lucid Motors was founded by Peter Rawlinson — the former chief engineer of the Tesla Model S. His mission: build the most advanced EV in the world. The Lucid Air delivered, winning MotorTrend Car of the Year and setting the EPA range record at 520 miles.

Lucid's secret is efficiency. Its 900V architecture and miniaturized drive units extract more miles per kWh than any other EV. The Air Grand Touring achieves this range without an absurdly large battery — just superior engineering.

The interior is a masterclass in luxury, with a floating 34-inch curved glass display, sustainable materials, and a cabin that feels more spacious than cars a class above. Lucid competes directly with Mercedes S-Class and BMW 7 Series.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Lucid Air really get 500+ miles of range?

+

Yes. The Air Grand Touring achieves 516 miles of EPA-rated range — the highest of any EV. Even the base Air Pure gets 430 miles. Lucid achieves this through industry-leading efficiency, not just a massive battery.

How does Lucid compare to Tesla?

+

Lucid Air surpasses Tesla Model S in range, interior luxury, and efficiency. Tesla has advantages in charging network, software ecosystem, price, and brand recognition. The Air Sapphire (1,234 hp) is faster than Model S Plaid.

Is Lucid financially stable?

+

Lucid is backed by Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund, which owns a majority stake. While Lucid burns cash as it scales, the PIF backing provides significant financial runway. Production is ramping but still below original targets.
