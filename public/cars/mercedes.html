About the Brand

The Best or Nothing — Now Electric

Mercedes-Benz brought its signature luxury and refinement to the EV world with the EQ lineup. The EQS sedan launched as the most aerodynamic production car ever made, with a drag coefficient of just 0.20.

The EQS features the stunning Hyperscreen — a 56-inch pillar-to-pillar curved display — and ride quality that rivals the S-Class. The EQE brings similar tech at a lower price, while the EQB offers an affordable entry point with three-row seating.

Mercedes also leads in battery warranty coverage with an industry-best 10-year / 155,000-mile warranty on EQ batteries, showing serious confidence in their electric powertrains.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Mercedes EQS worth it?

+

If you want the ultimate luxury EV experience, yes. The EQS offers ride quality, cabin quietness, and interior luxury that no other EV matches. The Hyperscreen is stunning. It competes with the Lucid Air and BMW i7.

What's the cheapest Mercedes EV?

+

The EQB starts at $49,900, making it the most affordable Mercedes-Benz EV. It's a compact SUV with three-row seating and up to 245 miles of range.

How long is Mercedes EV battery warranty?

+

Mercedes offers an industry-leading 10-year / 155,000-mile battery warranty on EQ vehicles — longer than Tesla (8yr/100-150k) and most competitors.
