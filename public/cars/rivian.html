About the Brand

Keep the World Adventurous Forever

Rivian launched with a bold promise: build the world's best adventure vehicles, and make them electric. The R1T pickup and R1S SUV delivered on that promise with quad-motor capability, tank-turn steering, and genuine off-road prowess.

Founded by RJ Scaringe in 2009, Rivian attracted major investment from Amazon and Ford before going public in one of the largest IPOs in history. The R1T was the first electric pickup to market, beating Tesla's Cybertruck and Ford's Lightning.

Rivian's vehicles stand apart with their outdoor-focused design, integrated camp kitchen option, gear tunnel storage, and a purpose-built Rivian Adventure Network of DC fast chargers in scenic locations.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Rivian use Tesla Superchargers?

+

Yes. 2025+ Rivian models have NACS ports. Earlier models get a free adapter. Rivian also has its own Adventure Network of fast chargers.

How does Rivian handle off-road?

+

Exceptionally well. Quad motors provide individual wheel torque control, adjustable air suspension offers up to 15 inches of ground clearance, and multiple off-road drive modes handle sand, snow, rock, and mud.

Is the R1T a good daily driver?

+

Absolutely. It drives like a luxury SUV on pavement — quiet, smooth, and fast. The 328-mile range handles most commutes for a week between charges.

What is the gear tunnel?

+

A lockable pass-through storage compartment between the cab and bed, unique to Rivian. It's perfect for camping gear, skis, or an optional slide-out camp kitchen.
