About the Brand

We Are Professional Grade — Electric

GMC revived the Hummer name as an all-electric supertruck, and it's as outrageous as you'd expect. The Hummer EV Pickup and SUV combine extreme off-road capability with EV technology to create something truly unique.

The Hummer EV features CrabWalk (diagonal driving via four-wheel steering), Extract Mode (6 inches of additional ground clearance), and a massive 200+ kWh battery for serious range. It can sprint 0-60 in approximately 3 seconds despite weighing over 9,000 lbs.

The Hummer EV isn't for everyone — it's massive, expensive, and weighs as much as a small building. But for those who want the most capable, attention-grabbing electric truck on the planet, nothing else comes close.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How heavy is the Hummer EV?

+

The Hummer EV Pickup weighs approximately 9,063 lbs — about 3x a Honda Civic. The massive weight is due to its 200+ kWh battery, body-on-frame construction, and reinforced off-road components.

What is CrabWalk?

+

CrabWalk uses four-wheel steering to move the Hummer diagonally — the rear wheels turn the same direction as the fronts. It's useful for tight trail maneuvers, parallel parking, and looking incredibly cool.

Is the Sierra EV better than the Silverado EV?

+

The Sierra EV is GMC's premium version of the same Ultium platform. It offers more luxury features, the MultiPro Tailgate, and available Denali trim. The Silverado EV is more affordable. Both share similar range and capability.
